Essential Travel Tips

  • Pack light, but smart. Always bring layers.
  • Keep digital and physical copies of your important documents.
  • Learn a few basic phrases in the local language.
  • Inform your bank about your travel plans to avoid card issues.
  • Stay hydrated and try local cuisine responsibly.
